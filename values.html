Kiwi Dose – The Vision

What It Is

Kiwi Dose is a vibrant juice and coffee bar that captures the natural freshness of New Zealand. We serve colourful, health-driven drinks and desserts in a modern, welcoming space that reflects Kiwi lifestyle. Our menu combines the best of both worlds – energizing juices packed with nutrients and premium coffee crafted to perfection.

Every element of Kiwi Dose, from our interior design to our product offerings, is thoughtfully curated to create an atmosphere that feels both modern and authentically Kiwi. We celebrate New Zealand's natural beauty and resources while providing a space that feels like home.

Our Vision of How

Through innovation, sustainability, and community connection, we aim to build Kiwi Dose into a trusted brand – first across Auckland, then nationwide, and ultimately on the global stage. By franchising, introducing drive-throughs, and entering the retail market, we see Kiwi Dose becoming the go-to name for a fresh dose of New Zealand, anywhere in the world.

We're committed to sustainable practices, supporting local suppliers, and minimizing our environmental impact. As we grow, we'll maintain our core values while expanding our reach, ensuring that the Kiwi Dose experience remains consistent and exceptional wherever you find us.
