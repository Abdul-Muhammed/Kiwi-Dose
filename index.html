Welcome to Kiwi Dose

Freshness Meets Flavor

Kiwi Dose is your destination for fresh, vibrant juices and premium coffee that capture the essence of New Zealand. We're more than just a juice bar – we're a community hub where freshness meets flavor, and every visit feels like a breath of fresh Kiwi air.

Located in the heart of Auckland, Kiwi Dose brings together the best of New Zealand's natural bounty. From our signature cold-pressed juices to our expertly crafted coffee blends, every product is designed to energize your day and nourish your body.

Your Daily Wellness

Whether you're starting your morning with a power-packed smoothie, grabbing a midday pick-me-up, or winding down with a refreshing juice blend, Kiwi Dose is here to be your daily dose of wellness and vitality.

Our menu features an array of cold-pressed juices, nutrient-rich smoothies, and premium coffee selections. Each drink is carefully crafted using locally sourced ingredients whenever possible, ensuring you get the freshest taste of New Zealand in every sip.
