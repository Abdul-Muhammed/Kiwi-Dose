Our Brand Story

Born from a love of fresh, natural goodness and the vibrant spirit of Aotearoa

"Kiwi Dose was born from a love of fresh, natural goodness and the belief that health should taste downright delicious."

The Beginning

Kiwi Dose was born from a love of fresh, natural goodness and the belief that health should taste downright delicious. Inspired by the vibrant landscapes and the laid-back, wholesome spirit of Aotearoa, every sip is a taste of New Zealand itself – pure, energising, and full of life.

The idea for Kiwi Dose came from recognizing a gap in the market – a place where health-conscious individuals and coffee lovers alike could come together. We wanted to create something that celebrated New Zealand's incredible natural resources while providing a modern, inviting atmosphere that felt like home.

Our founders, passionate about both wellness and community, envisioned a space that would serve as more than just a retail location. They dreamed of a hub where friends meet, ideas flow, and every visit leaves you feeling refreshed and energized.

Our Mission

More than a juice bar, we're creating a third space – a warm, welcoming spot where people can kick back, share a laugh, and have a good yarn over something fresh. Our vision extends beyond just serving great drinks; we're building a community around wellness, connection, and the simple joy of a perfectly crafted beverage.

At Kiwi Dose, our mission is simple: to deliver the freshest, most flavorful juices and coffee while fostering a sense of community and well-being. We source the finest ingredients, prioritize sustainability, and ensure every product meets our high standards for quality and taste.

We believe that taking care of your body shouldn't mean sacrificing flavor, and that's why we've dedicated ourselves to creating products that are both nutritious and absolutely delicious. From our cold-pressed juices packed with vitamins to our carefully selected coffee beans, every item on our menu is crafted with intention and care.

Looking Forward

As we prepare to open our doors in mid-December 2025, we're excited to become part of Auckland's vibrant food and beverage scene. We're committed to growing with our community, listening to feedback, and continuously innovating to bring you new and exciting flavors.

Our vision extends beyond our first location. We see Kiwi Dose expanding across New Zealand, bringing our unique blend of freshness, community, and innovation to cities nationwide. Through franchising, drive-through locations, and retail partnerships, we aim to make Kiwi Dose accessible to everyone who wants a daily dose of New Zealand's finest.
