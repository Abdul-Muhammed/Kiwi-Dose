Our Story

Rooted in Nature,
Driven by Passion

How a love for fresh, honest ingredients became KIWI DOSE.

Where It Began

Born from Aotearoa

KIWI DOSE started with a simple idea: what if every juice, every bowl, every bite could capture the pure, vibrant energy of New Zealand?

Inspired by the lush landscapes and clean air of Aotearoa, we set out to create something that felt as fresh and honest as the land itself. Not just another juice bar — but a genuine expression of natural goodness.

Our Mission

Real Food, Real Simple

We believe food should be honest. No artificial anything — just whole fruits, fresh vegetables, and natural ingredients combined with care and creativity.

Every item on our menu is designed to nourish your body and lift your day. We cold-press our juices, hand-build our bowls, and bake our desserts fresh — because shortcuts don't belong in good food.

What We Stand For

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do at KIWI DOSE.

Freshness First

Made fresh every day, never frozen. We source locally whenever possible to ensure peak flavour and nutrition in every serve.

Honest Ingredients

We're transparent about what goes into our food. Whole, real ingredients — nothing artificial, nothing hidden. What you see is what you get.

Sustainability

From compostable packaging to working with eco-conscious suppliers, we're committed to minimising our footprint on this beautiful land.

Community

KIWI DOSE is more than a brand — it's a gathering place. We believe in bringing people together over good food, great vibes, and genuine warmth.

Our Connection

Proudly Aotearoa

The silver fern in our logo isn't just a symbol — it represents our deep connection to this land. New Zealand's pristine environment, rich soil, and abundant produce inspire everything we create.

From the orchards of Hawke's Bay to the gardens of Canterbury, we celebrate the incredible bounty of Aotearoa in every cup and bowl.
